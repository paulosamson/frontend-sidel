Careers

Your chance to make a difference in an international dynamic company

A career at Sidel opens the doors to a global business environment full of opportunities for unrivalled growth where you can make a real difference. We believe in making Sidel a great place to work by empowering people and giving them the responsibility and the ownership to do amazing things.

 

Whether our people are field service engineers, technicians, researchers, product managers, bio-technologists, IT experts, packaging designers, financial controllers, communication specialists, receptionists, customer-facing sales professionals or customer-service support personnel, we all work together as a team to develop innovative and sustainable packaging solutions for all types of beverages. We are the best in the world at developing equipment and services that create beverage bottles made of the strong, fully recyclable and safe plastic material called PET that benefits our planet and creates great experiences for people around the world.
